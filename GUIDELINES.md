## App Overview

**Waris Genealogy** is a premium family tree management application designed for Malaysian families of all backgrounds. The app helps you:

- **Record and visualize** your complete family lineage across multiple generations
- **Calculate Islamic inheritance (Faraid)** distribution according to Syariah law
- **Check Mahram relationships** for Muslim marriage ceremonies
- **Manage family photos** and member profiles
- **Collaborate with family members** via email invitations
- **Backup and restore** your family data securely
- **Export printable reports** for family gatherings and documentation

The app supports **both Muslim and non-Muslim users**, with specialized tools for Islamic genealogy (Nasab) and traditional family tree visualization.

## Muslim-Specific Guidelines

### Faraid Calculator (Islamic Inheritance Distribution)

**What is Faraid?**
Faraid (الفرائض) is the Islamic law of inheritance that determines how a deceased person's estate is distributed among heirs according to Syariah principles.

**When to Use:**
- Planning estate distribution for a deceased family member
- Understanding inheritance rights in Islamic law
- Documenting inheritance calculations for family records

**How to Use:**
1. Go to **Tools** tab → **Faraid Calculator**
2. Select the **deceased person** from your family tree
3. The app automatically identifies eligible heirs:
   - Spouse(s)
   - Children
   - Parents
   - Siblings (if no children)
   - Other relatives (if applicable)
4. Enter the **total estate value** (optional, for calculation)
5. Tap **Calculate** to see the distribution:
   - Each heir's share (as fraction and percentage)
   - Calculated amount (if estate value entered)
6. Tap **Export as PDF** to save or print the report
7. Tap **Share** to email the report to family members

**Key Points:**
- Calculations follow **Sunni Islamic law** (Hanafi school)
- Spouse receives 1/4 or 1/8 depending on presence of children
- Children share equally (sons receive double daughters' share)
- Parents receive 1/6 each if children exist, otherwise 1/3
- Shares are calculated after debts, funeral costs, and bequests (up to 1/3)
- **Consult a Syariah scholar** for complex cases or non-standard situations

### Mahram Checker

**What is Mahram?**
Mahram (محرم) refers to relatives with whom marriage is forbidden under Islamic law. Understanding Mahram relationships is essential for:
- Determining marriage eligibility
- Wedding ceremony planning
- Understanding family relationships in Islamic context

**Mahram Categories:**
1. **Permanent Mahram** (cannot marry under any circumstances):
   - Parents, grandparents, and ancestors
   - Children, grandchildren, and descendants
   - Siblings (brother/sister)
   - Aunts and uncles
   - Nieces and nephews

2. **Temporary Mahram** (forbidden due to marriage bond):
   - Spouse's parents and in-laws
   - Spouse's children and step-children
   - Forbidden during marriage, but permissible after divorce/death

3. **Non-Mahram** (marriage is permissible):
   - Cousins (first, second, etc.)
   - Distant relatives beyond the above categories

**How to Use:**
1. Go to **Tools** tab → **Mahram Checker**
2. Select **Person A** and **Person B** from your family tree
3. Tap **Check Relationship**
4. Result shows:
   - **Mahram** (cannot marry)
   - **Non-Mahram** (can marry)
   - Relationship explanation (e.g., "First cousins")
5. Useful for wedding planning and Islamic compliance verification

**Important Notes:**
- Mahram rules apply to **Muslims only**
- Rules are based on **Sunni Islamic law**
- For Shia or other Islamic schools, consult a religious scholar
- **Consult a Qadi (Islamic judge)** for official marriage eligibility determination

### Naming Conventions for Muslim Families

**Malaysian Muslim Naming:**
- **Prefix/Title**: Hajj (Haji), Hajjah (Hajji), Dato', Tan Sri, Tun, Dr., Ir., etc.
- **First Name**: Personal name (e.g., Muhammad, Fatimah, Nur, Amin)
- **Bin/Binti**: Father's name (e.g., "Bin Abdullah" = son of Abdullah, "Binti Siti" = daughter of Siti)
- **Full Example**: Haji Muhammad Bin Abdullah

**How to Enter in Waris:**
- **Prefix/Title**: Select from dropdown (Hajj, Hajjah, Dr., Ir., etc.)
- **First Name**: Muhammad
- **Bin/Binti**: Abdullah
- **Result Display**: "Haji Muhammad Bin Abdullah"

## Data Management & Backup

### Backup Your Family Data

**Why Backup?**
- Protect against accidental data loss
- Transfer data to a new phone
- Share with family members
- Create permanent records

**Backup Methods:**

#### 1. **Google Drive Sync** (Recommended)
- Tap **Settings** → **Backup & Restore**
- Tap **"SEND TO GOOGLE DRIVE"**
- Select your Google account
- Backup file saved to Google Drive
- **To restore**: Tap **"DOWNLOAD FROM GOOGLE DRIVE"** and select the backup file

#### 2. **Email Sharing**
- Tap **Settings** → **Backup & Restore**
- Tap **"SEND VIA GMAIL / EMAIL"**
- Select email app (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
- Backup file attached to email
- Send to yourself or family members

#### 3. **Local Export/Import**
- **Export**: Tap **Settings** → **Backup & Restore** → **EXPORT**
- Backup saved to device storage (Downloads folder)
- **Import**: Tap **IMPORT** and select a previously exported JSON file

#### 4. **Auto-Sync** (Optional)
- Enable **"Auto sync on data changes"** toggle
- Automatically creates backup whenever you add/edit/delete members
- Useful for continuous protection

### Restore Your Family Data

**From Google Drive:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Backup & Restore**
2. Tap **"DOWNLOAD FROM GOOGLE DRIVE"**
3. Select the backup file from Google Drive
4. Confirm: "This will replace all current data"
5. Tap **Restore**
6. Restart the app to see restored data

**From Local File:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Backup & Restore**
2. Tap **IMPORT**
3. Select a JSON backup file from device storage
4. Confirm: "This will replace all current data"
5. Tap **Import**
6. Restart the app to see restored data

**Important:** Restoring data will **replace all current family data**. Always create a backup before restoring.

## Best Practices

### Building Your Family Tree

1. **Start with what you know**: Begin with yourself and immediate family
2. **Add one generation at a time**: Work systematically through generations
3. **Use accurate dates**: Verify birth dates with family documents
4. **Add photos**: Makes the tree more personal and memorable
5. **Keep relationships clear**: Ensure every member has at least one relationship link
6. **Invite family members**: Distribute data entry among family
7. **Regular backups**: Create backups monthly or after major updates

### Data Entry Tips

1. **Consistency in naming**: Use consistent spelling and format
2. **Complete information**: Fill in all available fields (birth date, place, etc.)
3. **Verify relationships**: Double-check marriage and parent-child links
4. **Use correct titles**: Include Hajj, Hajjah, Dato', etc. for accuracy
5. **Document sources**: Keep records of where information came from
6. **Update regularly**: Add new family members as they're born or married

### Privacy & Security

1. **Limit collaborators**: Only invite trusted family members
2. **Use appropriate roles**: Don't give Admin access unless necessary
3. **Backup regularly**: Protect against data loss
4. **Verify emails**: Confirm email addresses before sending invites
5. **Secure your device**: Use PIN/fingerprint lock on your phone
6. **Review permissions**: Periodically check who has access to your tree

### For Muslim Families

1. **Verify Faraid calculations**: Always consult a Syariah scholar for important decisions
2. **Document inheritance**: Keep Faraid reports for family records
3. **Update Mahram information**: Ensure relationship data is accurate for marriage eligibility
4. **Use correct naming**: Include Bin/Binti for proper Islamic naming convention
5. **Record religious information**: Note each person's religion for accurate Faraid calculations

## Troubleshooting

### Photo Upload Not Working

**Problem:** Photo picker crashes or "Failed to pick image from gallery"

**Solution:**
1. Ensure you have **gallery/camera permissions** enabled:
   - Android: Settings → Apps → Waris → Permissions → Camera/Gallery
   - iOS: Settings → Waris → Photos
2. Try selecting from **gallery first** (more reliable than camera)
3. Ensure photo file is **less than 5MB**
4. Try a different photo file
5. Restart the app and try again
6. If still failing, try **camera option** instead of gallery

### Backup/Restore Not Working

**Problem:** "Failed to export" or "Failed to import" error

**Solution:**
1. Check **storage permissions**:
   - Android: Settings → Apps → Waris → Permissions → Storage
   - iOS: Settings → Waris → Files and Folders
2. Ensure you have **sufficient storage space** on device
3. For Google Drive: Verify **Google account is logged in** on device
4. Try **local export/import** instead of Google Drive
5. Restart the app and try again
6. Check internet connection for Google Drive sync

### Google Drive Sync Not Working

**Problem:** "Failed to send to Google Drive" or "Failed to download"

**Solution:**
1. Check **internet connection** (WiFi or mobile data)
2. Verify **Google account is logged in**:
   - Android: Settings → Accounts → Google
   - iOS: Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud
3. Ensure **Google Drive app is installed** and updated
4. Try **email sharing** as alternative
5. Restart the app and try again
6. If issue persists, use **local export** to backup manually

### Family Member Not Showing in Tree

**Problem:** Added a member but they don't appear in the tree

**Solution:**
1. Verify the member has **at least one relationship link**:
   - Open member profile → Check "Family Connections"
   - If no links, tap "+ Parent", "+ Spouse", or "+ Child" to add one
2. Ensure the **relationship is saved**:
   - Tap Save after adding relationship
3. Refresh the tree:
   - Go to Home → Back to Tree
   - Or restart the app
4. Check if member is in **different tree root**:
   - Tap a family member → "View as Tree Root"
   - Navigate to find the missing member

### App Crashes on Startup

**Problem:** App crashes when opening

**Solution:**
1. **Force close** and restart:
   - Android: Settings → Apps → Waris → Force Stop → Reopen
   - iOS: Swipe up from bottom to close → Reopen
2. **Clear app cache**:
   - Android: Settings → Apps → Waris → Storage → Clear Cache
   - iOS: Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Waris → Offload App → Reinstall
3. **Restore from backup**:
   - If data is corrupted, restore from Google Drive or local backup
4. **Reinstall app**:
   - Uninstall and reinstall from Play Store/App Store
5. Contact support if issue persists

### Search Not Finding Members

**Problem:** Search doesn't return expected results

**Solution:**
1. Verify **spelling** of member name
2. Try **partial name search** (e.g., "Muham" for "Muhammad")
3. Check **language setting**:
   - If searching in English, ensure name is entered in English
   - Switch language and try again
4. Ensure member is **added to tree** (not deleted)
5. Refresh search by tapping search icon again
